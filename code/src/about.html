According to the National Restaurant Association, more people will review restaurant information online by using smartphones instead of computers. As a restaurant owner, having your own smart phone app is going to be as important as having your own website. It may already be! Restaurant owners are saying they want their own smartphone app, that is easy to use and is accessible.. eMunching is about simplicity and convenience for both the restaurant and its customers!

eMunching is an individually branded smartphone application for your restaurant and your customers. By helping you build customer loyalty, eMunching increases profits, and enables you to be at the cutting edge of the digital dining market and connect with your customers like never before. With Emunching, you can push specials and deals, accept and manage reservations, promote and market events and unlock additional revenue by promoting non-peak times and new dishes. You can also enhance your social media presence by facilitating
'likes' on your Facebook page.
